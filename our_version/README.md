# Zombie Bird

Zombie Bird is a fun, physics-based puzzle game where you launch heroic birds from a slingshot to defeat the evil zombies (pigs) and destroy their block fortresses!

## How to Play

1. **Launch Birds**: Click and drag on the slingshot to aim your bird, then release to launch.
2. **Abilities**: Some birds have special abilities! Press the **Left Mouse Button** or **Spacebar** while they are in the air to activate them:
   - **Basic Bird (Red)**: Just a classic, reliable bird.
   - **Dash Bird (Blue)**: Dashes forward at high speed!
   - **Explosive Bird (Black)**: Explodes on impact, dealing massive area damage!
   - **Split Bird (Yellow)**: Splits into three smaller birds to cover more ground!
3. **Win**: Destroy all the zombies in the level to win!
4. **Lose**: If you run out of birds before all zombies are defeated, you lose.

## Installation / Running

Just download the repository and double-click `HappyBird.exe` to play. 
(Note: Make sure the `Data/` folder is in the same directory as the executable so the game can load its assets and levels).

## Controls

- **Left Mouse Click & Drag**: Aim slingshot
- **Left Mouse Release**: Launch bird
- **Left Mouse Click / Spacebar**: Activate bird's special ability mid-flight
- **Enter / Left Click**: Navigate menus
